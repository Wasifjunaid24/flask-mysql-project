Order and Delivery

This section has all information regarding ordering process and delivery details

To process an order, one has to choose a product from different sections and sizes listed on the website that can be easily
navigated. Once, a product or multiple products is/are selected on its/their specific page/s, click on add to cart to move
forward.
Once, you have selected the product/s you want to purchase, click on the checkout button. This will lead you to another tab
where you need to fill out the necessary information (E-mail, Contact Information, and Shipping Address) to proceed.
Then, you will receive a confirmation of your order request through e-mail and text message. Lastly, our representative will
contact you in 48 hours to confirm your order and proceed to ship



The average delivery time is 05-07 days. Although it may take more or less time in case of time-specific circumstances



The status of delivery can be tracked by our customers through the order ID provided at the time of order placement.



We charge a flat 200/- PKR shipping rate per order.
