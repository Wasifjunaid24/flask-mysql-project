SHIPPING POLICY



WHEN WILL MY ORDER BE DELIVERED?



It takes us 4-5 business days to deliver your order to your doorsteps.Orders placed on Saturdays and Sundays, as well as any
gazetted holidays, will be processed the next business day.Orders may be delayed temporarily during peak hours or due to
delays in information verification. Orders placed through our online payment system are subject to a fraud check, and will be
processed further once our payment processor has cleared them. For all cash on delivery methods, payments will be collected
from the shipping address You will receive an email with tracking information once your product has been sent; please check
your junk/spam folders as well.



WHAT DOES SHIPPING COST?



If your order is less than PKR 2,500, you will be charged PKR 200. For orders over PKR 2,500, we offer free shipping
throughout Pakistan.



WHICH COURIER SERVICE DO YOU USE FOR DELIVERIES?



It is dependent on the delivery area and the package type. We will send you an e-mail with the courier information and tracking
details once the shipment has left our warehouse.



WHAT IF I AM NOT IN THE HOUSE WHEN THE ORDER IS DELIVERED?



The courier will send you an SMS with the total payment due. When it comes to self-collection, make sure you are there at home.
In the event of your absence, the parcel might be delivered to any designated person/family member.



ORDER CANCELLATION



For cash on delivery orders, we verify orders with a maximum of two confirmation calls. If your order is left unattended, it will be
cancelled. Orders can be cancelled at any time before they are completed at the request of the customer. Sclothers reserves the
right to cancel orders for any reason, including out of stock items, wrong mobile numbers, or technical faults.
